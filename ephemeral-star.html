Ephemeral Star - Concept game

    # Sandbox RPG | Physics game | Exploration #

*

Concept

Embark on a whimsical adventure in a vibrant sandbox world, centered around a dazzling prismatic island. Dive into the heart of daily island life as you explore, interact, and immerse yourself in the bustling community of quirky islanders. With a playful and endearing physics engine at your fingertips, engage in a myriad of tasks essential to island living: from hunting and gathering to cleaning, retail, and management. Every action is infused with humor and charm, making even the most mundane tasks a joyous experience. Forge meaningful connections with the islanders, each boasting their own unique personality waiting to be discovered. Through witty dialogue and clever interactions, influence the course of the islanders' lives and embark on fresh, inventive tasks that spark creativity and innovation. But time is ticking. In just ten days, the town faces an impending end. Harness your acquired wisdom and unlock the potential of your genetic chain to overcome the town's limitations, leading everyone towards a brighter future. It's up to you to navigate the challenges, bring hope back to the community, and emerge triumphant from the looming disaster. Welcome to a world where imagination knows no bounds, and every moment is an opportunity for adventure.

RESPONSIBILITIES

In EPHEMERAL STAR, I took the role of Lead Game Designer, Concept Artist, Level Designer, Environment Artist.

Concept/Narrative Design


* World Building and Concept Development: Craft the intricate tapestry of the game's world, from its geography to its lore and culture.



* Ritual Culture Creation: Delve into the depths of the game's culture, developing rituals and traditions that add depth and authenticity to the world.



* Character Creation and Design: Bring the game to life with 12 characters, each possessing unique physical abilities and social responsibilities.



* World Environment Design: Shape the natural environment of the game world, crafting landscapes, flora, and fauna that breathe life into the setting.

.

.

.

.

Game Design


* NPC Daily Task System: Design a dynamic system that assigns daily tasks to NPCs based on their roles and responsibilities within the game world.



* "Adoption" Relationship Mechanic: Introduce a basic game-mechanism and unique relationship between players and NPCs.



* Physical System Design: Develop and refine the gameplay mechanics governing the control of up to 8 NPCs.



* Upgrade System for Players: Implement an upgrade system that empowers players to enhance their abilities, equipment, or resources over time.

.

.

Level Design


* Environmental Whiteboxing: Develop detailed whitebox prototypes for walkable environments within the open-world.

* Open-World Environment Design: Utilize Unity to design and construct a vast open-world setting, encompassing diverse landscapes and natural environments.

* Character Creation and Design: Bring the game to life with 12 characters, each possessing unique physical abilities and social responsibilities.

* Village Environment and Villagers' Home Design: Conceptualize and whitebox the layout of village and architecture of the village, as well as individual homes for its inhabitants.

Technical Art


* Material/Texture Artistry: Develop visually appealing materials for characters and plants, complete with shaders to achieve desired visual effects..



* Environmental Design: Conceptualize and design the terrain of the island, including its topography, landscapes, and overall appearance. Design visual elements such as lighting, weather effects, and environmental ambiance.
