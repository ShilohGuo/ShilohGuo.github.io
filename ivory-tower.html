Game Playthrough

Concept

Situated in the northern region of the Asian continent, District 29 thrives on scientific research technology derived from the renowned [Ivory Tower Project]. Operating autonomously, they harness these advancements for their own purposes.

Diverging from the spire-shaped mechanical designs of the "Ivory Tower Project," District 29's signal amplifiers boast an eggshell-shaped construction, firmly grounded and of undisclosed quantity. The populace of District 29 comprises exclusively of children handpicked from within its borders, referred to by locals as the 'Eggs of the New World.'

Assuming the role of a prosecutor within the United Nations Guardians Armed Alliance (UGAA), you're dispatched to District 29 with a mandate to investigate and quell the looming threat of the [Nightmare] entity as thoroughly as possible.
